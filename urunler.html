Argus

A lightweight and modular system designed for rapid deployment in short-range reconnaissance missions. Its compact design ensures high agility and portability.

  • Uçuş süresi: 30 dakika
  • Kamera: Sabitleyicili 4K HD
  • Kontrol Menzili: 5 km
  • Manevra Kabiliyeti: Yüksek hızlı adaptif motorlar
3B Model

PURA 6719

In 2024, we achieved 8th place worldwide at the (Student Unmanned Aerial Systems) competition with our VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) unmanned aerial vehicle named "PURA 6719."

  • Navigasyon: GPS + IMU entegrasyonu
  • Uçuş modu: Özerk + Manuel geçersiz kılma
  • Menzil: Eve dönüş özelliğiyle 10 km
  • Stabilite: Yüksek rüzgar direnci

PURA 638

In 2023, we participated in the TEKNOFEST International Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Competition with our fixed-wing UAV named "PURA 638" and were awarded the Performance Award thanks to our successful performance

  • Maksimum Hız: 120 km/s
  • Veri Bağlantısı: Şifreli gerçek zamanlı video
  • Batarya: Akıllı güç yönetimi
  • Yapı: Karbon fiber gövde

PURA 1679

Designed for endurance and efficiency, Eclipse excels in long-range missions. It features solar-assisted flight and advanced telemetry for extended autonomy.

  • Uçuş Süresi: 2+ saat
  • Enerji: Güneş paneli destekli
  • Telemetri: Gelişmiş çok kanallı sistem
  • Yük Kapasitesi: 2,5 kg'a kadar
