İHA Mükemmelliğinde Öncü

PURA develops edge AI-integrated unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) designed for defense, reconnaissance, and critical infrastructure operations. Our platform architecture combines modular UAV hardware with embedded processing pipelines, enabling autonomous object detection, real-time telemetry, and mission-adaptive flight without reliance on external connections.

Current systems operate in fixed-wing, vertical take-off and landing (VTOL), and tethered drone configurations. Each platform is validated through rigorous field deployments and evaluations under international UAV standards, focusing on electronic warfare resilience, low RF signature, and rapid field deployment.
